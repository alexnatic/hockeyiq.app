Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

HockeyIQ is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the HockeyIQ mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and basic profile information.

Workout & Performance Data: HockeyIQ collects hockey-related workout data, including training sessions, exercise details, performance metrics (such as duration, intensity, heart rate, or calories burned if available), and progress history.

Device & Usage Data: We may collect limited device information (such as device type, operating system, and app version) and usage data to improve app performance and reliability.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Track and display your hockey workout performance and progress
  • Provide personalized training insights
  • Improve app features and functionality
  • Maintain account security and prevent misuse

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Sharing

We may use trusted third-party service providers (such as cloud storage or analytics services) to operate and improve HockeyIQ. These providers only access information necessary to perform their services and are obligated to protect your data.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Your Choices

You may update or delete your account information at any time within the app. If you wish to permanently delete your account and associated data, you may contact us at the email below.

Children’s Privacy

HockeyIQ is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at support@hockeyiq.app.
