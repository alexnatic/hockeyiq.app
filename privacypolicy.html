Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

HockeyIQ ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the HockeyIQ mobile application.

Information We Collect

HockeyIQ does not require user accounts.

We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal information.

Health Data

HockeyIQ integrates with Apple HealthKit to read and/or write hockey-related workout and health data (such as workout sessions, heart rate, or activity metrics), only with your explicit permission.

All HealthKit data is stored securely by Apple on your device. HockeyIQ does not store this data on external servers and does not transmit HealthKit data outside of your device.

We do not use HealthKit data for advertising, marketing, or data mining purposes.

Data Storage

Any data generated within the app is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain remote databases or cloud storage for user data.

Third-Party Services

HockeyIQ does not use third-party services to collect or store personal or health data.

Children’s Privacy

Because we do not collect personal information, HockeyIQ does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by revising the Effective Date above.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at support@hockeyiq.app.
